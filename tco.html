TCO Model (Total Cost of Ownership)

The Equinix TCO (Total Cost of Ownership) model is a financial tool designed specifically to help buyers and owners calculate the direct and indirect costs of owning Equinix products and services. This model considers all costs associated with the acquisition, ownership, and maintenance of Equinix solutions throughout their entire lifecycle, providing a clear view of the true cost of Equinix ownership.
